APPLICATION

Online application  ·  self-assessment form

Submit your online application and complete a baseline physical and psychological self-assessment. PLAN B's selection team reviews every applicant individually.

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA

  • Age 18-55
  • No critical chronic illness
  • Valid passport (min. 2 years remaining)
  • No active criminal record
  • Financially solvent — assets eligible for ALTTERA transfer
  • Psychologically stable (no active psychiatric treatment)
  • Next-of-kin declaration submitted
